Splootie on Christmas Eve

It's Christmas Eve and Splootie is in his house, dreaming about his beautiful Girlfiend Elephant named Priya (coincidence right?!). It had been a tiring day for Splootie. However, nothing, I mean NOTHING, could prepare Splootie for what was about to happen next to wake him up from his dream.

  • Find out what happened next!

Santa and Hagrid on a mission

Whilst Splootie was sleeping on his couch, dreaming about Priya, in a not-so far away distance was going to be the arrival of some unsuspecting visitors, who were just outside his door (The weirdest cross-over story, but go with it....).

  • What did Santa and Hagrid want?!

Gifts & The Letter

The door had burst open, and to Splootie's surprise, Hagrid, Santa and Hegwig were in his living room. There was no time for questions, Hagrid and Santa were on a mission! Hagrid told Splootie that he was the only one who could get this important letter to Priya that was super urgent to be delivered (which was great, as Splootie wanted to go to see Priya, so it was a win-win). Santa was just being lazy this Christmas and wanted give Priya her presents via Splootie. So Splootie quickly got up and got his stuff packed, carrying the essential items, such as his walking stick, the Letter and the Gift, and set off to see Priya for Christmas!

Splootie and the Gang

Whilst Splootie was making moves to see Priya, walking down the road on his merry way, he bumped into his friends Big-G Giraffe, and Lil Highlander-C, with a few of the other homies. Splootie explained what was going on, and the crew couldn't understand why Hagrid and Santa were kicking it together in this weird cross-over. However, not to waste time, Splootie told the guys that he had to go, and to that note, The guys didn't question further and accompanied Splootie on this adventure.

Wake up in the morning, time to go

Splootie had woken up on Christmas morning after his epic dance-off. His time with the homies had come to an end after last nights escapades. It was time to go, but this time alone. The homies wished him good luck on his travels, and with that Splootie was off.

Priya And Her Christmas Wait

Whilst all this travel was happening from Splootie. Priya was waiting on around on Christmas, bored. She had her picture of Splootie close-by, who she missed dearly. However, as she was looking out the window, she saw a purple figure in the distance. It couldn't be..... could it?! (well it was because you have Splootie's journey but be surprised!)

I see you!

Priya's eyes hadn't deceived her. As the dashing purple figure got closer, the smile grew on Priyas face. She knew her love had arrived, and she couldn't believe it! It was like it was Christmas gift (hehe hoho haha)

Honey, I'm home!

Splootie had made his way into Priya's house! He had the goods for her, and she was equally as shocked to see Splootie, but also ecstatic he was here for Christmas!
